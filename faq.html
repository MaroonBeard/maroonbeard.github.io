Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to reach out.

Simply head over to our Request a Quote page, fill out the form with your project details, and upload your artwork. We'll review everything and get back to you within 1–2 business days with a free, no-obligation quote.

We accept PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), PSD (Photoshop), and EPS files. For the best results, we recommend vector formats (SVG, AI, EPS) or high-resolution images (at least 300 DPI). If you're not sure about your file, send it over and we'll let you know!

Standard turnaround is typically 5–7 business days after artwork approval. Rush orders may be available depending on our current workload - just let us know your deadline and we'll do our best to accommodate.

While we primarily work with customer-provided artwork, we can help with basic design adjustments and setup. If you have an idea but need help bringing it to life, include a description in your quote request and we'll discuss options.

Pricing depends on several factors: the product type, quantity, number of print colors, size of the design, and any special requirements. That's why we offer free quotes - so we can give you an accurate price based on exactly what you need.

We specialize in t-shirts, hoodies, and other apparel, as well as banners, yard signs, posters, and various signage. If you have a custom project in mind that doesn't fit these categories, reach out - we love a challenge!

We offer both local pickup and shipping. Shipping costs and timelines will be included in your quote based on your location and order size.
